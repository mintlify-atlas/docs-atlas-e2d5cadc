title: Product Reviews

---

LarpLand includes a comprehensive review system that lets customers share their experiences and rate products.

## Viewing Product Reviews

Product reviews are accessible from the product detail page:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open product details">
    Tap any product card in the catalog to view its detail page
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scroll to reviews section">
    Below the product information, you'll find the "Comentarios y valoraciones" section
  </Step>
  <Step title="Read reviews">
    Browse through customer reviews, ratings, and comments
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Review Card Display

Each review is shown in a card with:

- **User avatar**: Circular badge with the reviewer's first initial
- **User name**: The reviewer's display name
- **Review date**: When the review was posted (formatted for readability)
- **Star rating**: Visual rating out of 5 stars (filled stars indicate the rating)
- **Review comment**: The full text of the customer's review

### Review Sorting

Reviews are displayed in reverse chronological order:

- Newest reviews appear first
- Older reviews appear below
- This ensures you see the most recent customer feedback

### Date Formatting

Review dates are displayed in a user-friendly format:

- **Today**: "Hoy, HH:MM" (e.g., "Hoy, 14:30")
- **Yesterday**: "Ayer, HH:MM" (e.g., "Ayer, 09:15")
- **Within a week**: "Hace X dias" (e.g., "Hace 3 dias")
- **Older**: DD/MM/YYYY format (e.g., "15/02/2026")

### Empty Review State

If a product has no reviews yet:

- A message displays: "Todavia no hay resenas para este producto."
- You can be the first to review the product

## Writing a Review

To submit a review for a product:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to product detail">
    Open the product detail page from the catalog
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scroll to review form">
    Below the existing reviews, you'll find the "Anade una resena" form
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your comment">
    Type your review in the multi-line text field (3 lines visible)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select rating">
    Choose a rating from 1 to 5 stars using the dropdown selector
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit review">
    Tap the "Enviar resena" button to post your review
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Review Form Fields

The review form includes:

- **Comment field** (required): Share your thoughts about the product
- **Rating dropdown** (required): Select from 1 to 5 stars
- **Submit button**: Sends your review with a confirmation message

### Rating Scale

Use the following rating guidelines:

- **5 stars**: Excellent, exceeded expectations
- **4 stars**: Very good, met expectations
- **3 stars**: Average, acceptable quality
- **2 stars**: Below average, some issues
- **1 star**: Poor, significant problems

## Review Validation

The review system enforces several rules:

### Required Fields

- **Comment**: You must enter text in the comment field
- Empty or whitespace-only comments are rejected
- An error message prompts: "Por favor, introduce un comentario"

## Submission Process

When you submit a review:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Validation">
    The form checks that all required fields are filled
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save review">
    Your review is saved with your user ID, product ID, comment, and rating
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update rating">
    The product's overall rating is recalculated automatically
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refresh display">
    The reviews list updates to show your new review
  </Step>
  <Step title="Form reset">
    The comment field clears, ready for future reviews on other products
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Processing Indicator

While submitting your review:

- The submit button shows a loading spinner
- Button text changes to "Enviando..."
- The button is disabled to prevent duplicate submissions

## Product Rating Updates

When you submit a review:

1. **Immediate calculation**: The system calculates the new average rating
2. **Automatic update**: The product's overall rating updates in real-time
3. **Visible changes**: The rating displayed on the product detail page refreshes
4. **Catalog sync**: The updated rating appears in the product catalog

### Rating Calculation

Product ratings are calculated as:

- Sum of all review ratings ÷ number of reviews
- Displayed to one decimal place (e.g., "4.3")
- Shown as "X / 5" format

## Review Display Components

### User Avatar

Review cards include a personalized avatar:

- Circular design with parchment background color (#F3EBD4)
- Displays the first letter of the reviewer's name
- Dark green text color (#2C4432)
- Fallback to "?" if the username is empty

### Star Rating Visualization

Ratings are displayed as:

- Filled stars (★) for the rating value
- Outlined stars (☆) for remaining stars up to 5
- Amber color for high visibility
- 18px size for readability

## Error Handling

### Review Loading Errors

If reviews fail to load:

- An error message displays with details
- The review form remains available
- You can still submit a review even if existing reviews won't load

### Submission Errors

If your review fails to submit:

1. An error snackbar appears with details
2. Your comment text is preserved in the form
3. The submit button re-enables so you can retry
4. Check your internet connection and try again

## Review Guidelines

For helpful, high-quality reviews:

- **Be specific**: Mention what you liked or disliked
- **Be honest**: Share your genuine experience
- **Be respectful**: Keep comments constructive
- **Be relevant**: Focus on the product itself

<Note>
  Reviews are displayed publicly to all LarpLand users. Write reviews that help others make informed purchasing decisions.
</Note>

## Review Lifecycle

### After Submission

Once you submit a review:

- It appears immediately in the reviews section
- Other users can see your review when viewing the product
- The product's average rating updates to reflect your input
- Your review remains associated with your user account

### Editing or Deleting

<Warning>
  The current version does not support editing or deleting reviews. Make sure your review is accurate before submitting.
</Warning>

## Best Practices

- Review products after you've had time to use them
- Provide context about your experience
- Mention how the product meets (or doesn't meet) its description
- Rate honestly to help other roleplayers make informed choices
